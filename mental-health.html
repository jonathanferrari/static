About Us

Our project proposes solutions to increase diversity in the field of mental health care. We aim to improve the representation of historically underrepresented racial groups in the US in the mental health care profession.

Why It Matters

The lack of diversity in the mental health care profession has real consequences. Patients from underrepresented groups may feel uncomfortable or unheard when seeking help, and may not receive the best care possible as a result. By increasing diversity in the field, we can help address these disparities and ensure that everyone has access to high-quality mental health care.

Our Proposal

Our proposal includes several key strategies to increase diversity in the mental health care profession, including:

  • Partnering with organizations that serve underrepresented communities to recruit and support diverse candidates for mental health care positions
  • Providing scholarships and other financial support to students from underrepresented groups who are pursuing mental health care careers
  • Offering training and mentorship programs to help underrepresented students succeed in mental health care programs
  • Encouraging mental health care organizations to adopt diversity and inclusion policies and practices
  • Increasing funding for research on diversity in mental health care and its impact on patient outcomes
